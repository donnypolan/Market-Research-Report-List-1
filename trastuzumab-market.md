<p><ul><li>Human Source</li><li>Animal Source</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Trastuzumab market can be segmented into human-source and animal-source types based on the origin of the antibodies used in its production. Human-source Trastuzumab is derived from genetically engineered human cells, offering higher specificity and reduced immunogenicity, making it a preferred option in cancer therapies, particularly for HER2-positive breast cancer. In contrast, animal-source Trastuzumab, derived from hybridoma technology using animal cells, may be less expensive but can pose greater immunogenic risks. Both sources cater to diverging market needs and patient responses.</p></p>
