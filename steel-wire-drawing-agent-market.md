<p><strong>The Steel Wire Drawing Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Based Lubricants</li><li>Water-Based Lubricant</li><li>Dry Wire Drawing Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel wire drawing agent market comprises three main types: oil-based lubricants, water-based lubricants, and dry wire drawing agents. Oil-based lubricants offer excellent lubrication and protection against corrosion, suitable for high-speed applications. Water-based lubricants are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and provide good cooling properties, making them ideal for less demanding processes. Dry wire drawing agents, often in powder form, reduce friction without liquid residues, suitable for applications requiring minimal contamination. Each type caters to specific operational needs in wire manufacturing.</p></p>
