<p><strong>The Electric Endoscopic Stapler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear</li><li>Standard</li><li>Circular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric endoscopic stapler market is categorized into three main types: linear, standard, and circular staplers. Linear staplers are designed for creating straight or angled tissue closures, ideal for resection and anastomosis procedures. Standard staplers are used for various general surgical applications, providing versatile stapling solutions. Circular staplers, on the other hand, are utilized primarily for end-to-end or side-to-side anastomosis, offering a more specialized approach for gastrointestinal surgeries. Each type caters to specific surgical needs and enhances operational efficiency.</p></p>
