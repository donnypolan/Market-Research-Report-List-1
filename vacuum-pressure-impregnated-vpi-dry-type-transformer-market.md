<p><p>The Vacuum Pressure Impregnated (VPI) dry-type transformer market is categorized based on cooling methods: Air Natural (AN) and Air Forced (AF). AN transformers rely on natural airflow for cooling, allowing heat dissipation without additional mechanical assistance, making them efficient and cost-effective. In contrast, AF transformers utilize mechanical fans to enhance airflow, providing better heat management and supporting higher loads. These cooling methods influence the transformers' applications, efficiency, and overall performance in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
