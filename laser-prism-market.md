<p><ul><li>Right Angle Prism</li><li>Wedge Prism</li><li>Penta Prism</li><li>Amici Roof Prism</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser prism market encompasses various types, including right angle prisms, wedge prisms, penta prisms, and Amici roof prisms. Right angle prisms are utilized for total internal reflection, while wedge prisms introduce a controlled deviation in light paths. Penta prisms reflect light at a 90-degree angle, ensuring consistent orientation, and are often used in surveying instruments. Amici roof prisms, designed for compact viewing optics, convert images while maintaining correct orientation, making them crucial in optical devices for enhanced imaging performance.</p></p>
