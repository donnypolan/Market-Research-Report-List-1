<p><p>The advanced conductive agents market primarily includes Carbon Nanotubes (CNTs) and Graphene, both known for their exceptional electrical conductivity and mechanical properties. CNTs are cylindrical nanostructures that enhance the performance of composites and electronic devices due to their high strength-to-weight ratio and excellent thermal conductivity. Graphene, a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a hexagonal lattice, boasts remarkable electrical conductivity and flexibility, making it ideal for applications in flexible electronics, energy storage, and advanced coatings. Both materials are essential for innovating next-generation technologies.</p></p>
