<p><p>The RF power semiconductor market encompasses various components crucial for radio frequency applications. RF power amplifiers enhance signal strength for transmission, while RF passives, like capacitors and inductors, manage signal flow without amplification. RF duplexers enable simultaneous sending and receiving of signals on a single channel, facilitating efficient communication. RF switches control signal paths between different components, ensuring optimal routing. Other RF devices include filters and mixers, contributing to a comprehensive infrastructure for wireless communication and signal processing.</p></p>
