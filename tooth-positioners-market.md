<p><strong>The Tooth Positioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-extraction Positioners</li><li>Extraction Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tooth positioners market is divided into two main types: non-extraction positioners and extraction positioners. Non-extraction positioners are used in orthodontics to align teeth without the need for tooth removal, focusing on maximizing dental aesthetics and function by maneuvering existing teeth into optimal positions. On the other hand, extraction positioners are designed for cases where one or more teeth must be removed to achieve the desired alignment, facilitating a more comprehensive realignment strategy that considers the resulting space.</p></p>
