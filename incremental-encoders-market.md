<p><strong>The Incremental Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Rotary Encoders</li><li>Incremental Linear Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The incremental encoders market is primarily segmented into two types: incremental rotary encoders and incremental linear encoders. Incremental rotary encoders are used to measure angular position and provide feedback for applications such as motors and robotics. They generate pulses as the shaft rotates, indicating movement direction and speed. Incremental linear encoders measure linear displacement, commonly utilized in automation, CNC machines, and 3D printing. Both types enhance precision and control in various industrial and technological applications, driving demand across multiple sectors.</p></p>
