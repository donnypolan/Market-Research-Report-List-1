<p><p>The microSD card market is categorized into three main types: microSD, microSDHC, and microSDXC. microSD cards, typically up to 2GB, cater to basic storage needs. microSDHC (Secure Digital High Capacity) ranges from 2GB to 32GB, supporting higher performance for applications like video recording. microSDXC (Secure Digital Extended Capacity) offers greater storage, from 32GB to 2TB, making it ideal for high-resolution photos and 4K videos. This differentiation allows users to select cards based on storage needs and device compatibility.</p></p>
