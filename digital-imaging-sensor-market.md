<p><p>The digital imaging sensor market is primarily categorized into two main types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors. CCD sensors are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, making them ideal for professional cameras and scientific applications. In contrast, CMOS sensors are more energy-efficient, allowing for faster processing speeds and integration into compact devices like smartphones. The market trends often reflect a shift towards CMOS technology due to its versatility and cost-effectiveness in consumer electronics.</p></p>
