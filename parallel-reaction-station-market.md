<p><ul><li>Single Control System</li><li>Multiple Control Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Parallel Reaction Station market is categorized into two types: Single Control System and Multiple Control Systems. A Single Control System manages all reactions from a central unit, simplifying operations and monitoring. In contrast, Multiple Control Systems feature individual controls for each reaction, allowing greater flexibility, customization, and simultaneous experimentation. While Single Control Systems are often more cost-effective and easier to operate, Multiple Control Systems cater to complex processes requiring diverse conditions or extensive research, appealing to advanced laboratories.</p></p>
