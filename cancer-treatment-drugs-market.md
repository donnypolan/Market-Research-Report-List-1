<p><p>The cancer treatment drugs market encompasses several types, each addressing the disease's unique characteristics. Chemotherapy utilizes cytotoxic agents to target rapidly dividing cells. Hormonal therapy, or biologic therapy, blocks hormones that fuel certain cancers. Other therapies may include stem cell treatments and novel drug combinations, broadening the scope of options for personalized cancer care.</p></p>
