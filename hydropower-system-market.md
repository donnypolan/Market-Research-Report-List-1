<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Hydropower System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Turbines</li><li>Reaction Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydropower system market includes impulse and reaction turbines, each serving distinct applications. Impulse turbines, such as Pelton wheels, utilize the energy of falling water to drive the turbine blades directly, ideal for high-head installations. In contrast, reaction turbines, like Francis and Kaplan turbines, operate submerged in water, generating lift through the water's flow and pressure, suitable for low to medium head sites. Growing demand for renewable energy drives interest in these turbine types, shaping market dynamics.</p></p>
