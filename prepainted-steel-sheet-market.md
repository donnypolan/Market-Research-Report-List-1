<p><ul><li>PE Prepainted</li><li>HDP Prepainted</li><li>SMP Prepainted</li><li>PVDF Prepainted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prepainted steel sheet market encompasses various types based on coating technologies: PE prepainted steel features a polyester coating, offering good durability and color retention for general applications. HDP prepainted steel utilizes high-durability polymer coatings for enhanced weather resistance and is suitable for exterior use. SMP prepainted steel employs silicone-modified polyester for improved gloss and UV resistance, while PVDF prepainted steel has a polyvinylidene fluoride coating, providing superior durability and chemical resistance, ideal for demanding environments.</p></p>
