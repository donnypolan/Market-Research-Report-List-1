<p><p>The power metering market comprises three main types: analog meters, digital meters, and smart meters. Analog meters are traditional devices that measure power consumption using mechanical dials. Digital meters offer enhanced accuracy and display readings electronically. Smart meters represent the latest innovation, enabling two-way communication between the utility and consumers, allowing for real-time monitoring, remote management, and data analytics. This evolution from analog to smart technology enhances energy management and supports the growing demand for efficient energy consumption practices.</p></p>
