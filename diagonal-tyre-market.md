<p><ul><li>Common Diagonal Tyre</li><li>Bias Belted Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diagonal tyre market includes two main types: common diagonal tyres and bias belted tyres. Common diagonal tyres, characterized by their crisscrossing tread patterns, offer flexibility and enhanced grip, making them suitable for various vehicles. Bias belted tyres, on the other hand, incorporate a belt layer beneath the tread, providing improved stability and durability while maintaining some flexibility. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with common diagonal tyres favored for light-duty applications and bias belted tyres preferred for heavier vehicles.</p></p>
